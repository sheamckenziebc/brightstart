Navigate Clinic Development with Confidence

Launching or expanding your practice shouldn't be a puzzle. BrightStart Group addresses the common hurdles with integrated solutions:

Site Selection & Pre-Lease Due Diligence

Is it a shell or a retrofit? We provide thorough site evaluations and "fit-for-purpose" reports to ensure your chosen location meets all regulatory and operational needs before you sign the lease.

Compliant Design & Permitting

Our interdisciplinary design team (IDT) specializes in healthcare. We navigate complex workflow zoning (on-stage/off-stage), operatory layouts, and HVAC infection-control requirements, ensuring your plans are approved and optimized.

Specialized Financing & Leasing

Avoid cash-flow strain during ramp-up. Our banking and leasing partners are healthcare-focused lenders, bundling leaseholds, equipment (with 100% financing options like Prime - 0.25%), and working-capital lines of credit.
