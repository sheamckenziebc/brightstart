Leasing

Michael Permack

Michael Permack is the founder and Managing Partner of Spire Group, Canada's premier real estate advisory firm for healthcare professionals. With over 4,100 completed assignments, Michael has been a transformative advocate, ensuring healthcare practitioners secure optimal practice spaces through expert lease and purchase negotiations. Motivated by witnessing unfair treatment of healthcare professionals in the 1990s, he built Spire to champion their interests, saving clients over $600 million to date.

A long-time cancer survivor, Michael has been recognized with the Medal of Courage and a Governor General's Award for raising $550 million for the Canadian Cancer Society. He lives in Victoria with his wife, three children, their partners, and a grandchild.

Spire Group

Spire Group specializes in real estate solutions for healthcare professionals across Canada. Their comprehensive services include site selection with demographic and competitive analysis, negotiation of offers and leases, lease renewals, construction support, and landlord issue resolution. This is foundational to our site feasibility process.
