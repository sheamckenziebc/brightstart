Our Streamlined Clinic Development Process

BrightStart Group guides you through a proven 6-step process, ensuring clarity, efficiency, and successful outcomes for your new or renovated clinic. We handle the complexities of each stage, from initial site feasibility to your grand opening day.

Your Journey with BrightStart Group:

  1. 1

    Site Feasibility & Pre-Lease Due Diligence

    We evaluate potential locations for compliance with relevant standards, existing conditions ("shell vs. retrofit"), and overall suitability, providing "fit-for-purpose" reports to inform your leasing decisions, supported by experts like Spire Group.

  2. 2

    Concept Design & Workflow Planning

    Our interdisciplinary design team (IDT) translates your vision into a functional concept, focusing on patient flow optimisation, infection-control zoning, and operatory layouts tailored to your practice.

  3. 3

    Permits & Tenant Improvements (TI)

    We manage the creation of detailed architectural and engineering drawings, secure necessary permits (including College of Physicians & Surgeons of BC reviews), and oversee the turn-key tenant improvement construction, managed by partners like CarePoint Builders, adhering to critical path scheduling.

  4. 4

    Equipment Installation & Integration

    Coordinating with suppliers like Sinclair Dental, we manage the procurement, delivery, and installation of all medical/dental equipment, ensuring seamless integration with the central plant and operatory designs.

  5. 5

    Funding Close & Financial Coordination

    Working with our banking partners, we finalize all financing arrangements, including draws for leasehold improvements, equipment leases (often 100% financing), and working-capital lines of credit to ensure smooth financial transitions.

  6. 6

    Grand Opening & Post-Occupancy Support

    The final step is your successful grand opening! We ensure all systems are go and provide post-occupancy support to address any immediate needs as you begin serving patients in your new, state-of-the-art clinic.
